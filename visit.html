Park Information

Services

Accessibilty Access
  • All rides except Whitewater are wheel-chair accessible.
  • Vans and other large vehicles can use the Special Access Parking lot upon request.
  • There are ramps in the park, ramps over a 12 rise-to-run ratio have nearby lifts.
  • If you require special transportion within the park, please call guest services before your arrival.
